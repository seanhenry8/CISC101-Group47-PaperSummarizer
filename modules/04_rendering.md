# Final report rendering

#### Compilation order
1. **Paper summary**
2. **Section-by-section table**
3. **Expert vs. lay summary**
4. **Mini-glossary**
5. **Checks and warnings**
6. **Optional appendices:** Citations and equation explanations if requested or available.

#### Formatting rules
- **Headers:** Use hierarchical headings (#–####) with sentence case.
- **Bold lead-ins:** Bold key labels in bullets and numbered steps (e.g., **Main idea:**).
- **Tables:** Use compact tables for section overviews; avoid more than 5 columns.
- **Clarity:** Short paragraphs (3–5 sentences); avoid dense text blocks.
- **Child-friendly emphasis:** Lay summary comes before expert summary and uses simpler language.

#### Rendering templates

##### Paper summary
- **Big idea:** One paragraph in simple language.
- **Main findings:** 3–5 bullets emphasizing what changed or was discovered.
- **Takeaway:** One sentence a child could repeat.
